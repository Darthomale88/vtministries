Our Beliefs

The Bible

II Peter 1:20-21

WE BELIEVE the Bible is the inspired, infallible word of God and the authority of salvation and holy living written by holy men of old as they were moved by the Holy Spirit

God

I Corinthians 8:6, II Corinthians 5:19, Ephesians 4:6, Joel 2:28

WE BELIEVE one God who is infinite in power, holy in nature, attributes and purpose; omniscient, omnipotent and omnipresent. We believe that He was revealed to us; Father in creation, Son in redemption and Holy Spirit in His comforting grace that never leaves those who trust in Him.

Jesus Christ

Deuteronomy 6:4, Ephesians 4:4-6, Colossians 2:9, I Timothy 3:16

WE BELIEVE there is one God, Jesus Christ, who is God manifested in the flesh. He is both God and man.

The Gospel

I Corinthians 15:1-4, Acts 2:4, 37-39, Romans 6:3

WE BELIEVE the saving gospel is the good news that Jesus died for our sins, was buried and rose again. We obey the gospel (II Thessalonians 1:8, I Peter 4:17) by repentance (death to sin), water baptism in the name of Jesus Christ (burial), and the baptism of the Holy Spirit with the initial sign of speaking in tongues as the Spirit gives the utterance (resurrection).

The HolyGhost

Acts 2:14

WE BELIEVE in the death, burial and resurrection of Jesus. We believe that He ascended on high and has sent His Spirit which in the Holy Ghost. The Holy Ghost was poured out in Jerusalem over 2000 years ago and is still filling the hearts of those who diligently seek Him today.

Bishop William L. Harris Sr. D.D.
